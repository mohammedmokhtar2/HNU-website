## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### For Best Results:

1. **Message Length**: Keep it between 300-500 words
   - Long enough to be meaningful
   - Short enough to maintain attention

2. **Image Quality**: 
   - Use professional headshot (800x800px recommended)
   - Clear background or university setting
   - Good lighting and sharp focus

3. **Tone**: 
   - Welcoming and inspirational
   - Professional but approachable
   - Focus on vision and values

4. **Update Frequency**: 
   - Refresh at the start of each academic year
   - Update for major announcements
   - Keep content current and relevant

5. **Bilingual Balance**:
   - Ensure both languages convey the same sentiment
   - Adapt for cultural context, not just translate
   - Maintain consistent tone in both versions
